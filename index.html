High-scoring phrases (discourse markers & hedging)

    Part 1 & 2 tips

    • Part 1 (interview): don't stop at yes/no — extend each answer by 2–3 sentences; back up opinions with concrete examples; show range of vocabulary while staying natural.
    • Part 2 (collaborative task): actively invite your partner to speak ("How do you see it?"); don't dominate or fall silent; practise disagreeing politely ("I see it slightly differently…").
    • All parts: a steady pace beats a fast one; use fillers ("That's an interesting way of putting it…") to buy thinking time when you pause.
